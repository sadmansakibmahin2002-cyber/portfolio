IEEE Conference Paper

An Explainable Machine Learning Approach to Multiclass Diabetes Risk Assessment

Authors: Sharmin Sultana Akhi, A.S.M. Shakil Ahamed, Md. Rana Miah, Md. Sadman Sakib Mahin, Md. Samiul Alom

This IEEE conference paper presents an explainable machine learning framework for multiclass diabetes risk assessment. Eight machine learning models were evaluated, with Random Forest and XGBoost achieving the highest accuracy (98.11%). Explainable AI techniques including SHAP, PDP, and TreeInterpreter were used to improve transparency and support clinical decision-making.
